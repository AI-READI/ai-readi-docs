**The following variables are not in the published dataset and require Data Access Committee approval and a Data Use Agreement before they can be accessed:**
 - 5-digit zip code
 - Medications
 - Race/ethnicity
 - Sex

**Planned for future release:**

 - Past health records
 - Genetic information

**Bio-repository samples are available for research upon request and appropriate funding. This will also require a Data Use Agreement and Material Transfer Agreement. To request access to controlled variables or bio-repository samples, please send an email to [aireadi-dac@ohsu.edu](mailto:aireadi-dac@ohsu.edu).**
